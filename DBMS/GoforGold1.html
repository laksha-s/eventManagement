111 - Basketball (Men)

The Men's Basketball competition is a thrilling showcase of skill, speed, and teamwork on the hardwood. Teams of five players dribble, pass, and shoot their way to victory, aiming to score points by getting the ball through the opponent's hoop. This fast-paced game demands agility, strategy, and a great deal of athleticism.
